Circuitous City

Circuitous City is a collection of works that considers the circuit created betwen a sender and receiver of information. It acknowledges the role the creators and owners of circuits play in imbuing them with values (whether capitalist, imperialist or otherwise). With this in mind, it asks how we as users can create circuits—however long, slow, inconvenient or circuitous they may be—to foster the kinds of values that we wish to reproduce in our communities and society.

Visitors to this site can participate by opting to receive copies of the works made for Circuitous City, delivered in person by the artist throughout the course of the exhibition.

Digital versions of the postcards, as well as reports from the the 'circuits' traversed en route to the in-person deliveries will be documented via regular blog updates to the CORRESPONDENCE page on this site, and emailed to those who wish to sign up for the mailing list.
